### **Direct School Admission (DSA)**
#### **Direct School Admission – Sec (DSA-Sec) for 2023**
We invite all interested 2022 Primary 6 students to apply for Beatty Secondary School via the DSA-Sec Exercise.

Parents and students are encouraged to choose schools based on the student’s aptitudes and strengths, while keeping in mind the schools’ academic and non-academic requirements, and the programmes that are available to develop their potential and talents.

Students who are successfully admitted to the school via DSA are expected to honour their commitment to the school and participate in the activities related to the talent area they are selected for from Year 1 – 4.

##### **Low Cheng Lum Scholarship**
Students with Achievement Level (AL) 13 or better will be awarded the Low Cheng Lum Scholarship, worth a total value of S$2500, based on the following terms and conditions:

*   Cash Award of $1000 in Secondary 1, for students with AL 13 or better; and
*   Cash Award of $500 in Secondary 2-4, subject to good performance and conduct in the preceding year.

**Application (5 May 2022, 11am to 1 June 2022, 3pm)**<br>
All applicants can apply for DSA-SEC through the centralized DSA-Sec Portal.

1\.  **For applicants studying in MOE mainstream primary schools:** Parent may log in directly to DSA-Sec Portal. Only one parent’s Singpass is required. Parents/guardians who are unable to apply online can approach their child’s primary school for assistance.

2\.  **For applicants NOT studying in MOE mainstream primary schools:** They need to apply for a Registration Number (RGxxxxxxx) to access the DSA-Sec Portal. For more details, please refer to MOE website at: [https://go.gov.sg/nmsdsa-sec-application](https://go.gov.sg/nmsdsa-sec-application) .

3\.  Students can indicate **up to three choices** (maximum of two choices for the same school under two different talent areas).

4\.  Applicants studying in mainstream primary schools will have their primary school information shared directly with DSA-Sec schools that they apply to. It is optional to indicate non-school-based activities and achievements.

5\.  Given the prevailing national posture, DSA-Sec schools will be conducting **in-person selection**, with the prevailing Safe Management Measures (SMMs) in place to ensure the safety of students and staff. Selection trials and interviews will be conducted for shortlisted applicants between 27 June and 12 September 2022.  More details will be furnished nearer the dates.

6\.  **Application Timeline**
