<h4><strong>(2026 Cohort) Secondary One – Appeal for Transfer to Beatty Secondary School </strong></h4>
<h4><strong>Information to note:</strong></h4>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Primary 6 students who have just received their secondary school posting
in 2025 and wish to appeal for a transfer to Beatty Secondary School should
note that their PSLE score <strong>must meet the school's cut-off point of the posting year</strong>,
which will be published in SchoolFinder after the release of the Secondary
One Posting results.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Transfer will be <strong>subject to available vacancies</strong> and the <strong>school’s admission criteria</strong>. 
 </p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The <strong>deadline</strong> for the submission of appeals is on <strong>26 December 2025, 12pm</strong>.
Late submission will not be considered.   </p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The application is <strong>deemed</strong> to be <strong>unsuccessfu</strong>l
if you do not hear from the school by <strong>2 January 2026, 5pm</strong>. The
school does not disclose reasons for selection / non-selection of students.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students <strong>should still report to their posted secondary school</strong> on <strong>2 January 2026</strong> until
the <strong>transfer is approved</strong>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>For more information, please refer to MOE’s website:</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p><a href="https://www.moe.gov.sg/secondary/s1-posting/results/appeal-for-school-transfer" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.moe.gov.sg/secondary/s1-posting/results/appeal-for-school-transfer</a>
</p>
<h4><strong>Instructions: </strong></h4>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Go to <a href="https://go.gov.sg/2026s1appeal-bty" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://go.gov.sg/2026s1appeal-bty</a> (Opens
on Friday, 19 December, 8:00 a.m.)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Fill out the online form accurately, and ensure you upload all required,
supporting documents. </p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Appeals with incomplete forms, information and/or missing documents will
not be considered.</p>
</li>
</ol>
